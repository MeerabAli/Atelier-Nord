About Us

Crafting Spaces
That Inspire

Founded in 2008 in Copenhagen, Atelier Nord has grown from a small design studio into an internationally recognized architecture firm. Our work spans residential, commercial, and urban projects across Europe and beyond.

Rooted in Nordic design principles, we believe in simplicity, sustainability, and the transformative power of exceptional design. Every project is an opportunity to create something meaningful—spaces that resonate with their inhabitants and enrich their communities.

Our Philosophy

Architecture as
Living Art

We approach each project as a unique dialogue between site, program, and human experience. Our design process is deeply collaborative, involving clients, consultants, and craftspeople from concept to completion.

Sustainability is not an add-on but a fundamental principle. We design for longevity, adaptability, and environmental responsibility, creating buildings that will serve future generations with grace and efficiency.

Meet the Founder

Søren Nielsen

Principal Architect & Founder

With over 25 years of architectural experience, Søren founded Atelier Nord with a vision to create architecture that transcends the ordinary. Trained at the Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts and having worked with Foster + Partners and Snøhetta, he brings a wealth of international experience to every project.

Søren's work has been published in Architectural Review, Domus, and Dezeen, and he is a frequent speaker at international design conferences. His passion lies in creating spaces that enhance human wellbeing while respecting the natural environment.
